Origin of chess

Most historians agree that chess began in India in 6th century AD Its roots began as a game called "Chaturanga".

The name "Chaturanga" respresented the four branches of the Indian army at the time there being known as: Infantry, Cavalry, Elephants, and Chariots

These four divisons at the time are what evolved slowly into the 6 chess pieces known today
